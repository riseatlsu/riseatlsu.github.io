Mission

Robotics and Software Engineering are transforming the global economy. Around the world, industries are adopting robots faster than ever, but the United States still lags behind other major economies (e.g., China). Even within the country, most robots are concentrated in a few regions like California, Texas, and the Midwest. States such as Louisiana remain on the sidelines, with limited access to automation and a workforce that hasn't yet benefited from this technological shift.

At the RISE Lab, we believe this gap represents an opportunity for research. By combining robotics with software engineering, we are helping to create a solid literature on how to design, program, and deploy intelligent systems that can improve local industries and communities in Louisiana and abroad. We believe the robotics revolution should not be limited to a few regions or industries. It should lift every community, empower every student, and open new opportunities for economical growth right here in Louisiana. Join us on this fight!
